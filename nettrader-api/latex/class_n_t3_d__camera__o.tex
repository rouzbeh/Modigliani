\subsubsection{Detailed Description}
Idea: Its position in space is determined by the translation vector of the parent class. The camera is rotated and moved like any \hyperlink{class_n_t3_d__object__o}{NT3D\_\-object\_\-o} object, where the rotation describes the direction in which the imaginary lens is pointed (default the positive X axis). Under all rotations the upper border of the camera \char`\"{}picture\char`\"{} is parallel to a predefined direction (default is the positive Y axis).

Usage: The camera abtraction is based on a appropriate transformation of the projection and the model view matrices.
